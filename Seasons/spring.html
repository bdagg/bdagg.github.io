The Seasons

Spring is the time of rebirth. Trees, plants and animals are all out and lively as colors are seen everywhere. When I think of spring I remember the cherry blossoms at the University of Washington in Seattle, where I am from. The weather is starting to warm up so you begin to see people coming out and being active again.
