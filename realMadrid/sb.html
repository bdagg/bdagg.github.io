Santiago Bernabéu Stadium

Here is an image of Real Madrids stadium in Spain.

One of the biggest stadiums in the world, home to one of the biggest clubs in the world.

After rennovations, the Santiago Bernabéu seats a capacity of 81,044. It has been the home stadium of Real Madrid since 1947. It is the second largest stadium in Spain, after Barcelona's Camp Nou.
